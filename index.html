4th of July Celebration & Parade

Parade July 3, festivities July 3 evening through July 4 . Downtown Warrenville (parade) & Cerny Park

Two days of hometown celebration. The parade steps off on July 3, followed by festivities at Cerny Park that evening and all day on the Fourth, capped by a fireworks show. The 2026 celebration is centered at Cerny Park, so plan your spot.
