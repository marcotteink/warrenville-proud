4th of July Parade & Celebration

Parade July 3 at 6:00 PM, Cerny Park festivities through July 4 . Parade from Warren Avenue & Tracy Place to Cerny Park

Warrenville's two-day hometown celebration. The parade steps off downtown at 6:00 on July 3 and rolls to Cerny Park for an evening of festivities (6:00 to 11:00 PM). The fun picks back up on the Fourth with a kids parade and live music from noon right up to the fireworks. The 2026 celebration is centered at Cerny Park, so plan your spot.
